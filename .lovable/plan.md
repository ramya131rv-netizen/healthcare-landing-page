

# EyeLevel Growth Studio — Healthcare Marketing Landing Page

## Overview
A single-page, luxury premium healthcare marketing website for EyeLevel Growth Studio, Chennai. Dark navy aesthetic with blue-to-teal accent gradients, GSAP scroll animations, and full MCI-compliant messaging.

## Design System
- **Theme**: Dark navy hero with subtle grid background and soft radial glows
- **Colors**: Deep navy base, blue → teal/green gradient accents
- **Typography**: Space Grotesk (headings), Inter (body)
- **Cards**: Rounded 12–16px corners, soft shadows, clean spacing
- **Animations**: GSAP-powered — stagger fade-ups, scroll-trigger reveals, count-up stats, smooth accordion, navbar shrink on scroll
